Postal Colours

Well, this is one of my best experiments with postal colours. The objective of the painting was to potray 'PERMANENCE', and in my opinion there's no match for the permanence of nature. I have tried to display bad, stormy weather. But a tree can even withstand such a cruel weather with all its glory remaining indifferent.

This painting is made, keeping in mind the tonal variations in the real world. The appropriate combination of the tonal variations and the corresponding contrast (wherever needed) gives us something really pleasing to the eyes, as is the case here.
